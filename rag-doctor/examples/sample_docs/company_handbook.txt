AI Product Team Handbook

The team builds internal AI tools for knowledge search, customer support, and document question answering.

Every answer generated by an AI assistant should include citations when the answer depends on internal documents.

Before shipping an LLM feature, the team evaluates retrieval quality, answer faithfulness, latency, cost, and failure cases.

For sensitive or high-risk domains, the assistant should say when context is missing instead of guessing.

团队要求所有知识库问答功能都要保留引用来源。没有足够上下文时，系统应该提示上下文不足，而不是编造答案。
